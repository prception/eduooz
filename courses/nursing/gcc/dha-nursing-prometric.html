What is the DHA Nursing Licensing Exam?

GCC Nursing – DHA Nursing Licensing Exam is a premier international nursing licensure program focused on preparation for the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) Nursing Licensing Exam for nurses who wish to pursue careers in the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region. The DHA Nursing License is one of the most recognized GCC nursing credentials required to work as a Registered Nurse (RN) in the United Arab Emirates, particularly in Dubai's healthcare sector.

01

Register Through Sheryan Portal

Candidates create an account and submit their application through the DHA Sheryan Licensing System. Personal, academic, and professional details including nursing qualifications and home country registration must be submitted accurately.

02

Exam Booking

Eligible candidates schedule and book the DHA Prometric Computer-Based Test (CBT) at an authorized testing center. The Prometric authorization code is provided by DHA after initial application review.

03

DataFlow Verification

Candidates complete Primary Source Verification (PSV) through DataFlow to verify educational qualifications, nursing registration, professional experience, and other supporting documents directly with the issuing institutions.

04

Receive Eligibility Letter

Upon successful verification and approval, candidates receive the DHA Eligibility Letter. This allows them to seek employment in DHA-approved healthcare facilities across Dubai.

05

Employment and Licensing Activation

After securing employment with a DHA-approved healthcare institution, the DHA nursing license can be activated according to DHA regulations. The nurse is then authorized to practice in Dubai's healthcare sector.

Everything you need to know.

From curriculum specifics to international placement assistance, find the answers to your most pressing questions.

The DHA Nursing Prometric Exam is a licensing examination conducted by the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) to assess the eligibility of nurses who wish to work in Dubai, UAE healthcare facilities. It is a mandatory exam for obtaining a DHA nursing license.

DHA Nursing Licensing is the official approval granted by the Dubai Health Authority that allows qualified nurses to practice legally in Dubai hospitals, clinics, and healthcare centers after passing the DHA Prometric Exam.

Candidates with GNM Nursing, B.Sc Nursing, Post Basic B.Sc Nursing, or equivalent nursing qualifications along with relevant clinical experience are eligible to apply for the DHA Nursing Prometric Exam.

The minimum qualification generally includes General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM), B.Sc Nursing, or higher nursing degrees recognized by the relevant nursing council in the candidate's home country.

Yes, most DHA Nursing categories require at least 2 years of clinical experience after qualification. However, requirements may vary depending on the nursing role and DHA regulations.

Candidates can apply online through the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) Sheryan portal by creating an account, submitting documents, paying fees, and scheduling the Prometric exam.

The DHA Nursing license is generally valid for 1 to 2 years and must be renewed as per Dubai Health Authority regulations.

DataFlow is a primary source verification process required by DHA to validate educational certificates, nursing registration, and work experience before appearing for the exam.

Applicants need educational certificates, passport copy, nursing registration certificate, experience certificates, passport-size photograph, and other identity documents.

After passing the DHA Nursing Exam, candidates can work as Staff Nurses, Registered Nurses, ICU Nurses, Emergency Nurses, and specialized nurses in Dubai hospitals.

The DHA Nursing Exam provides international career opportunities, tax-free salary, global exposure, professional growth, and stable healthcare employment in Dubai.
