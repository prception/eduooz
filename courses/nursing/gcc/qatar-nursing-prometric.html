What is Qatar Nursing Prometric?

GCC Nursing – Qatar Nursing Prometric Licensing Exam is a leading international nursing licensure preparation program designed for nurses aiming to practice in Qatar and other Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries. The Qatar Prometric Nursing License is required to work as a Registered Nurse (RN) or Licensed Nurse (LN) in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities across Qatar.

01

Exam Booking

Candidates register and schedule their Qatar Nursing Prometric Examination through the authorized Prometric system. Personal, academic, and professional details must be submitted accurately during registration.

02

DataFlow Submission

Candidates complete Primary Source Verification (PSV) through DataFlow to verify educational qualifications, nursing registration, professional experience, and all supporting documents required for Qatar licensing.

03

Eligibility Confirmation

Following successful DataFlow verification, candidates receive eligibility approval from the Qatar healthcare licensing authority (QCHP). This confirms the candidate meets all requirements to practice nursing in Qatar.

04

Receive Final Eligibility Letter

Candidates obtain the official eligibility letter required for employment and licensing procedures within Qatar. This letter is presented to QCHP-approved hospitals and healthcare facilities when seeking employment.

05

Final Licensing

After securing employment and completing all regulatory requirements, candidates receive their professional nursing license to practice in Qatar. The license is valid for 1 year and must be renewed annually.
