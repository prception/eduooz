About This Exam

Understanding NORCET.

What is AIIMS NORCET?

AIIMS NORCET (Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test) is a national-level examination conducted by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi. It serves as the single gateway for recruitment of Nursing Officers across all AIIMS institutes in India.

The exam tests candidates on their clinical nursing knowledge, patient care fundamentals, and critical thinking abilities. Successful candidates are posted as Nursing Officers (Group B, Non-Gazetted) with attractive pay scales, government benefits, and career growth opportunities.

With over 3,000+ vacancies announced annually across 25+ AIIMS institutes, NORCET represents one of the most prestigious nursing career pathways in India, offering job security, pension benefits, and international recognition.

Who Can Apply & Age Criteria

Eligibility & Age Requirements.

Eligibility Explorer

Educational Qualification

B.Sc. Nursing (4 years) from a recognized university OR Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing OR Diploma in General Nursing & Midwifery (GNM) with minimum 2 years clinical experience in a 50+ bed hospital.

Registration Requirement

Must hold valid and active Registration as Nurse & Midwife (RN/RM) from any State Nursing Council of India. Registration must be valid on the date of appointment.

Clinical Experience

B.Sc. Nursing graduates: No experience required. GNM Diploma holders: Minimum 2 years of clinical nursing experience in a recognized hospital with 50 or more beds.

Age Limit

Minimum 18 years and maximum 30 years for General category (as on the last date of application). Age relaxation applicable for reserved categories as per Government of India norms.

Nationality

Must be an Indian citizen. OCI/PIO card holders are not eligible for this recruitment. Candidates must fulfill domicile requirements for specific AIIMS institutes if applicable.

Medical Fitness

Candidates must be medically fit as per the standards prescribed by AIIMS. A medical fitness certificate from a registered medical practitioner will be required at the time of joining.

Age Relaxation Guide

General Category

30 years

No age relaxation. Upper age limit is 30 years as on the last date of online application submission.

SC / ST

35 years

+5 years relaxation over General category. Applicable to candidates belonging to Scheduled Caste / Scheduled Tribe communities.

OBC (Non-Creamy Layer)

33 years

+3 years relaxation over General category. Must possess valid Non-Creamy Layer certificate issued by competent authority.

Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD)

40 years

+10 years relaxation over General category. Must have minimum 40% disability certified by designated medical board.

Ex-Servicemen / Defence Candidates

35 years

+5 years relaxation (after deducting military service period). Applicable to discharged defence personnel and dependents.

Widows / Divorced Women

35 years

+5 years relaxation applicable to widows and judicially divorced women who have not remarried.

Other Reserved Categories

Varies

Departmental candidates and other special categories as per AIIMS notification. Refer to official advertisement for specific relaxations.

    Success Roadmap

    How To Prepare.

    Understand the Exam Pattern and Syllabus

    Begin by thoroughly studying the AIIMS NORCET exam pattern — 200 MCQs in 90 minutes with 1/3rd negative marking. Map out subject-wise weightage and identify high-yield areas. Download the official syllabus and create a structured study plan covering all 6 major nursing subjects.

    Week 1-2

    Focus on Core Nursing Subjects

    Dedicate focused study time to Medical-Surgical Nursing, Community Health Nursing, OBG, Pediatrics, Mental Health Nursing, and Nursing Foundations. Use clinical case-based learning, visual aids, and mnemonics for better retention of complex concepts. Prioritize topics that appear repeatedly in previous papers.

    Weeks 3-10

    Practice Mock Tests and Previous Papers

    Solve at least 5 years of previous AIIMS NORCET papers to understand question trends, difficulty levels, and frequently tested topics. Take full-length CBT mock tests under timed conditions to build exam stamina and improve time management skills.

    Weeks 11-16

    Review Regularly and Reinforce Key Concepts

    Schedule periodic revision sessions to reinforce learned concepts. Use flashcards, quick revision notes, and spaced repetition techniques. Analyze your mock test performance to identify weak areas and allocate extra revision time to those subjects.

    Weeks 17-20

    Get Expert Guidance and Mentorship

    Join a structured coaching program with experienced NORCET-qualified faculty. Leverage 1-on-1 mentorship for personalized strategy sessions, doubt clearing, and performance analysis. An expert mentor can help you optimize your preparation in the final crucial weeks.

    01

    Online Application Submission

    Candidates must complete the online application through the official AIIMS NORCET portal during the registration period. Personal, academic, and professional details must be submitted accurately.

    02

    Application Verification / Scrutiny

    Submitted applications are reviewed to verify eligibility criteria, educational qualifications, nursing registration, and other required details.

    03

    Admit Card / Hall Ticket Download

    Eligible candidates can download their AIIMS NORCET Admit Card from the official portal before the examination date.

    04

    Computer-Based Test (CBT)

    Candidates appear for the online CBT examination conducted by AIIMS. The test evaluates nursing knowledge, clinical understanding, aptitude, and professional competency.

    05

    Result / Merit List Publication

    Based on examination performance and eligibility criteria, AIIMS publishes the merit list and examination results.

    06

    Document Verification & Counselling

    Shortlisted candidates are called for document verification. Educational certificates, nursing registration certificates, category certificates, and supporting documents are verified.

    07

    Final Appointment & Posting

    Selected candidates are appointed as Nursing Officers in AIIMS institutions across India according to merit rank, reservation norms, and available vacancies.
