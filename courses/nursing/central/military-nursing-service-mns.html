01

Online Application Submission

Candidates must apply online through the official Indian Army / Join Indian Army portal during the notified registration period, filling in personal, academic, and professional details accurately.

02

Application Fee Payment

Pay the prescribed application fee online as per category. Successful fee payment is required to confirm and complete the registration process for the MNS exam.

03

Admit Card Download

Eligible candidates can download their MNS Admit Card from the official portal before the examination date. The admit card is mandatory for entry into the examination hall.

04

Written Examination

Candidates appear for the written exam which assesses nursing knowledge, general awareness, aptitude, and English proficiency. The exam is conducted at designated centres across India.

05

Merit List & Shortlisting

Based on written exam performance, a merit list is published. Shortlisted candidates are called for the next stage — medical examination and document verification as per Army norms.

06

Medical Examination & Document Verification

Shortlisted candidates undergo a thorough medical examination as per Indian Army standards. Original documents including educational certificates, nursing registration, and category certificates are verified.

07

Final Merit List & Commission

The final merit list is prepared based on written exam scores, medical fitness, and document verification. Selected candidates are granted Short Service Commission as Nursing Officers in the Military Nursing Service, Indian Army.
