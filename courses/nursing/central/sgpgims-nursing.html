What is SGPGIMS Entrance Exam?

The Central Government SGPGIMS Exam refers to the entrance examination conducted by the Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGIMS) for admission into its PG medical, nursing, and allied health science programs. SGPGIMS is one of India's premier central government funded medical institutes, located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, and it conducts its own competitive entrance tests for various courses including MD, MS, DM, MCh, M.Sc Nursing, and allied health disciplines.

01

Online Application Submission

Candidates must apply online through the official SGPGIMS portal during the notified application period, filling in personal, academic, and professional details.

02

Application Fee Payment

Pay the application fee online according to the category or program (UR/OBC/EWS/SC/ST). Payment is required to complete the application process.

03

Application Status & Document Upload

After successful registration, candidates can check application status and upload scanned photographs, signatures, and other required documents as per SGPGIMS guidelines.

04

Entrance Examination (CBT / Written Test)

Eligible candidates appear for the Computer-Based Test (CBT) or written examination on the scheduled date. The examination assesses subject knowledge relevant to the chosen program, including medical, nursing, or allied health disciplines.

05

Shortlisting for Merit List / Score Publication

Based on examination performance, candidates are shortlisted for counselling, admission, or further selection stages. Scores or merit lists are published on the official SGPGIMS website.

06

Document Verification & Medical Examination

Shortlisted candidates must produce original documents for verification and may undergo a medical fitness examination if required by the program.

07

Final Merit List & Admission

The final merit list is prepared based on examination scores, document verification, and medical fitness. Selected candidates are admitted to SGPGIMS courses according to merit, reservation policies, and available seats.

Everything you need to know.

From exam eligibility to study strategies, find answers to your most pressing SGPGIMS Nursing Officer preparation questions.

The SGPGIMS Nursing Officer Exam is a competitive recruitment examination conducted by Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGIMS) for the recruitment of Nursing Officers in its hospitals and healthcare facilities. SGPGIMS is one of India's premier medical institutions and regularly recruits nursing professionals through a Common Recruitment Test (CRT).

Candidates possessing B.Sc Nursing, Post Basic B.Sc Nursing, GNM Nursing, or equivalent qualifications along with valid Nursing Council registration are generally eligible to apply, subject to the latest recruitment notification.

The educational qualification for SGPGIMS Nursing Officer vacancies typically includes B.Sc Nursing, Post Basic B.Sc Nursing, or GNM with relevant eligibility requirements and Nursing Council registration.

Candidates can apply online through the official SGPGIMS recruitment portal by registering, filling out the application form, uploading required documents, and paying the prescribed application fee before the last date.

The SGPGIMS Nursing Officer selection process generally includes a Computer-Based Test (CBT), merit-based evaluation, document verification, and medical fitness verification.

The SGPGIMS Nursing Officer Exam is usually conducted as a Computer-Based Test (CBT) consisting of objective-type multiple-choice questions covering nursing subjects and aptitude-related topics.

Applicants generally need educational certificates, Nursing Council registration certificate, identity proof, age proof, category certificate (if applicable), passport-size photograph, signature, and other documents mentioned in the notification.

SGPGIMS Nursing Officer jobs offer government job security, attractive salary packages, healthcare benefits, paid leave, professional growth opportunities, and career advancement in a prestigious medical institution.

Nursing Officers can advance to Senior Nursing Officer, Nursing Superintendent, Assistant Nursing Superintendent, Deputy Nursing Superintendent, Nursing Administrator, and other leadership positions within the healthcare system.

SGPGIMS Nursing Officer jobs offer excellent career opportunities in a premier medical institution, competitive salary packages, government benefits, professional development, and long-term job stability.
