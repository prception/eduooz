What is NVS School Staff Nurse?

The NVS School Staff Nurse Recruitment Exam is a central government recruitment examination conducted by the Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) to appoint Staff Nurses / School Nurses for the network of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) across India.

Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti is an autonomous body under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, known for providing quality residential education in rural areas through its chain of schools. The Staff Nurse position plays a critical role in maintaining health, first aid, preventive care, and emergency response services for students and school staff.

01

Online Application Submission

Candidates must apply online through the official NVS portal during the notified application period, filling in personal, academic, and professional details.

02

Application Fee Payment

Pay the application fee online according to the post or category (UR/OBC/EWS/SC/ST/Ex-Servicemen). Payment is required to complete the application process.

03

Application Status & Document Upload

After successful registration, candidates can check application status and upload scanned photographs, signatures, and other required documents as per NVS guidelines.

04

Entrance Examination (CBT / Written Test)

Eligible candidates appear for the Computer-Based Test (CBT) or written examination on the scheduled date. The examination assesses nursing knowledge including medical-surgical nursing, community health, mental health, and school health nursing.

05

Shortlisting for Merit List / Score Publication

Based on examination performance, candidates are shortlisted for counselling or appointment. Scores or merit lists are published on the official NVS website.

06

Document Verification & Medical Examination

Shortlisted candidates must produce original documents for verification and may undergo a medical fitness examination if required for the post.

07

Final Merit List & Appointment

The final merit list is prepared based on examination scores, document verification, and medical fitness. Selected candidates are appointed to Staff Nurse positions in Navodaya Vidyalaya Schools according to merit, reservation, and available vacancies.

Everything you need to know.

From exam eligibility to study strategies, find answers to your most pressing NVS Staff Nurse preparation questions.

NVS School Staff Nurse Recruitment is a recruitment process conducted by the Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) to hire qualified Staff Nurses for Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) across India. Selected candidates provide healthcare services to students and staff within residential school campuses.

Candidates possessing B.Sc Nursing, GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery), or equivalent nursing qualifications from a recognized institution along with valid Nursing Council registration are generally eligible to apply.

The educational qualification for NVS Staff Nurse vacancies generally includes B.Sc Nursing, Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM), or equivalent nursing qualifications recognized by the Indian Nursing Council.

The selection process generally includes a Computer-Based Test (CBT), written examination, document verification, and medical fitness assessment depending on the recruitment notification.

Yes, valid registration with the State Nursing Council or Indian Nursing Council is generally mandatory for NVS School Staff Nurse eligibility.

Applicants generally need educational certificates, Nursing Council registration certificate, identity proof, age proof, category certificate (if applicable), passport-size photographs, signature, and other supporting documents.

NVS School Staff Nurses provide first aid, emergency medical care, student health monitoring, medication administration, health record maintenance, health education, and wellness support within residential school campuses.

NVS Staff Nurse jobs offer government job security, attractive salary packages, accommodation benefits (where applicable), healthcare facilities, paid leave, and career growth opportunities.

NVS Staff Nurses can advance to senior nursing roles, healthcare administration positions, school health program coordinators, and other healthcare supervisory positions based on experience and qualifications.

NVS School Staff Nurse jobs offer stable government employment, competitive salaries, residential school healthcare experience, attractive benefits, and opportunities to contribute to student health and well-being.
