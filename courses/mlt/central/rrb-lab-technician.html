About the RRB MLT EXAM

RRB MLT Exam is a government recruitment examination conducted by the Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) for the selection of eligible candidates for Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT) posts under Indian Railways healthcare services. The examination provides an opportunity for qualified laboratory professionals to work in railway hospitals, medical laboratories, and healthcare facilities.

The RRB MLT Exam evaluates candidates' knowledge and skills in Medical Laboratory Technology, including clinical pathology, microbiology, biochemistry, hematology, laboratory procedures, and diagnostic techniques.

01

Online Application Submission

Candidates submit their applications through the official Railway Recruitment Board portal with personal, educational, and required details.

02

Application Verification

Applications are checked against eligibility criteria, educational qualifications, age requirements, and other conditions stated in the RRB notification.

03

Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Eligible candidates appear for the RRB Computer-Based Test. The exam may cover General Awareness, Professional Ability, General Arithmetic, General Intelligence and Reasoning, and subject-specific Medical Laboratory Technology topics.

04

Shortlisting and Merit List Preparation

Candidates are shortlisted based on CBT performance and the selection criteria prescribed in the recruitment notification.

05

Document Verification

Shortlisted candidates must produce original documents for verification of educational qualifications, registration, identity, category claims, and other required credentials.

06

Medical Fitness Examination

Selected candidates undergo a medical examination according to the medical fitness standards prescribed by Indian Railways.

07

Final Appointment

Final selection is completed based on CBT performance, document verification, medical fitness, eligibility compliance, and applicable RRB recruitment rules.
