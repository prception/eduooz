About the KERALA PSC DHS MLT EXAM

Kerala PSC DHS MLT Exam is a government recruitment examination conducted by the Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC) for the selection of qualified candidates for Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT) posts under the Department of Health Services (DHS), Kerala. The exam provides opportunities for eligible candidates to build a career in government healthcare institutions across Kerala.

The Kerala DHS MLT Exam evaluates candidates' knowledge and skills in medical laboratory technology, including areas such as clinical pathology, microbiology, biochemistry, hematology, and laboratory procedures.

Submitted applications are checked against eligibility criteria, qualifications, age requirements, registration conditions, and other rules in the DHS notification.

03

Written Examination

Eligible candidates appear for the Kerala PSC written examination covering Medical Laboratory Technology subjects and other areas specified in the official syllabus.

04

Shortlisting and Rank List Preparation

Candidates are shortlisted based on examination performance and applicable Kerala PSC ranking criteria.

05

Certificate Verification

Shortlisted candidates must produce original certificates for verification of educational qualifications, professional registration, identity, category claims, and other required credentials.

06

Medical Fitness Verification

Selected candidates may undergo medical fitness assessment according to applicable government healthcare service requirements.

07

Final Appointment

Final selection and appointment are completed based on rank list position, eligibility, certificate verification, medical fitness, reservation rules, and DHS recruitment conditions.
