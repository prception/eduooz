What is RRB Pharmacist?

The RRB Pharmacist Exam is a competitive recruitment examination conducted by the Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB) under the Ministry of Railways, Government of India. It is held to select qualified candidates for the position of Pharmacist in various Zonal Railways and Production Units. The role includes providing pharmaceutical services, maintaining medicine stocks, and assisting in the hospital/health unit dispensary.

01

Application Submission

Candidates must first register and submit their applications through the official RRB online portal within the specified application period. The process generally includes online registration, personal and educational details, Pharmacy registration details, document upload, application fee payment (where applicable), and final submission.

02

Screening of Applications

Submitted applications are screened by the concerned RRB to verify eligibility based on educational qualifications, age, category, Pharmacy registration, and other recruitment conditions. Applications that do not satisfy the prescribed eligibility requirements may be rejected.

03

Computer Based Test (CBT)

Eligible candidates are called for the Computer Based Test, which may assess Professional Pharmacy Subjects, General Awareness, General Arithmetic, General Intelligence, and Reasoning Ability. The professional pharmacy section is a major part of preparation for the Pharmacist post.

04

Document Verification

Candidates who qualify based on CBT performance may be called for Document Verification, including educational certificates, D.Pharm or B.Pharm certificates, Pharmacy Council registration certificate, date of birth proof, category certificate (if applicable), identity documents, and other supporting certificates.

05

Medical Examination

Candidates must undergo the prescribed medical examination to confirm that they satisfy the medical standards required by Indian Railways for the Pharmacist post.

06

Final Merit List and Appointment

The final merit list is prepared based on CBT performance, successful document verification, medical fitness, and compliance with eligibility conditions. Selected candidates are then considered for appointment as Pharmacists within Indian Railways.

Everything you need to know.

From eligibility and syllabus specifics to the selection process and career benefits, find the answers to your most pressing questions.

The RRB Pharmacist Exam is a competitive recruitment examination conducted by the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) for selecting eligible candidates for Pharmacist posts in Indian Railways hospitals and medical units.

Candidates with Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm), Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), or Pharm.D from a recognized institution along with valid Pharmacy Council registration are eligible to apply.

The minimum qualification is D.Pharm or B.Pharm from a PCI-approved institution with valid registration in the State Pharmacy Council or Pharmacy Council of India.

The selection process generally includes a Computer-Based Test (CBT), document verification, medical examination, and merit list preparation.

The exam pattern generally includes objective-type questions covering pharmacy subjects, general science, general awareness, reasoning ability, and arithmetic.

An RRB Pharmacist is responsible for dispensing medicines, maintaining drug inventory, checking prescriptions, patient counseling, and ensuring safe medication practices in railway hospitals.

Applicants generally need educational certificates, pharmacy registration certificate, identity proof, age proof, category certificate (if applicable), passport-size photograph, and signature.

RRB Pharmacist jobs offer central government employment, railway medical benefits, job security, pension benefits, and attractive salary packages.

Important skills include pharmaceutical knowledge, hospital pharmacy management, drug dispensing accuracy, patient counseling, and a strong understanding of medicine safety.

RRB Pharmacist jobs offer stable central government employment, railway healthcare exposure, attractive salary, job security, and long-term career growth opportunities.
